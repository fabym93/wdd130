About Us - ThunderFalls White Water

We are a company with over 12 years of experience guiding safe and memorable whitewater adventures. We are dedicated to turning every ride into a story of excitement and connection with nature.

History

Thunderfalls White Water was born from the vision of two friends passionate about adventure and rafting excursions. In 2010, after a whitewater expedition, they realized that extreme experiences could go hand in hand with environmental responsibility. They decided to combine their love of nature with engineering reliable equipment and founded Thunderfalls White Water with the mission of organizing and coordinating safe and memorable rafting excursions for explorers of all levels. With a small operations center by the river and the support of experienced guides, the company grew thanks to innovation in logistics, ongoing training, and a strong aquatic ecosystem conservation program. Today, Thunderfalls White Water continues to expand its rafting routes, maintaining its spirit of adventure and environmental commitment, and accompanying each group on journeys that connect communities, rivers, and unforgettable emotions..
